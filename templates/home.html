Overview

IngeNUity Any discussion about the student life at
NU is incomplete without the mention of ingeNUity, NIIT University's
annual techno-cultural fest. The event is fully organized,
coordinated, and executed by the students, and hence displays
the best of all talent at NU. In its fifth year
now, ingeNUity is a three day event featuring more than 40 events, including,
technical events by the Microsoft Innovation Centre at NU.
The fest receives extensive participation from colleges in Delhi NCR and Jaipur.

Inter-batch competitions

A more informal type of competition, the batches of the
university indulge in friendly competition in various sports.
The events are help in the campus to get more interact with
the higher students and all the sport are included in this
competation and anyone can register and participate in the events.

Clubs

NU has numerous clubs, most of them self-organised by
the students themselves to meet their extra and co-curricular
interests. Club activities are regularly organised on
campus and off-campus as well
